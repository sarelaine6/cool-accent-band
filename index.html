Who We Are

Original music from Denver, Colorado.

Meeting in 2016 while playing in a local wedding band, Erica and Sarai formed an instant friendship and musical alliance. They began singing and songwriting together, feeling out their bright, snappy sound along the way. Erica brought her love of pop heroes like Ben Folds, Sia, and HAIM to her songwriting, while Sarai served up her powerful voice, inspired by rock greats like Hayley Williams (Paramore), Ann Wilson (Heart), and Freddie Mercury (Queen). While honing their electric performance in basements and at local venues, the band self-released their debut album “Burn This City” in March 2020. After a year of navigating the ever-changing music industry during the pandemic, they are excited to release the new, long-anticipated single “Say Goodbye” for the summer of 2021.
